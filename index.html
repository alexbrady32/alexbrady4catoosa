Why I'm Running

I’m running for County Commissioner because I believe our government should work for all of us—not just for a select few. We need leaders who understand the challenges faced by ordinary people and who are committed to building a community where everyone has the chance to thrive. I believe in the democratic process, and I want to ensure that every citizen in our county has a choice when as they go to vote.

My Vision for Catoosa County

It’s time for open and transparent government, where decisions are made with input from everyone. We need affordable housing solutions, improved infrastructure, and leadership that listens. I will be a good steward of taxpayer money, ensuring that spending is done in an equitable manner. And I will work with people from all backgrounds to find solutions that move our county forward together.

I’m not running to make promises I can’t keep—what I offer is my character of integrity. Together, we can create a Catoosa County that works for all of us.

About Me

My name is Alex Brady, and I grew up right here in NW Georgia. I live in Graysville with my wife and our two young children. As a software engineer, I lead a team that works with the armed forces to improve the lives of service members and their families. I hold degrees in Computer Science from Southern Adventist University and the Georgia Institute of Technology.
